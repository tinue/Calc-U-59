Relive the golden age of programmable calculators with a cycle-accurate emulation of the Texas Instruments TI-59, TI-58, and TI-58C — the iconic machines that brought real computing power to scientists, engineers, and students in the late 1970s and 1980s.

ACCURATE EMULATION
Calc-U-59 emulates the TMC0501 processor at the cycle level, reproducing the exact behaviour of the original hardware. The TI-58C's constant memory is preserved across restarts, just like the real thing. The Master Library ROM is included.

MAGNETIC CARD READER
Simulate the TI-59's magnetic card reader. Save and load programs and data registers as card files, stored on your device and synced via iCloud across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

PAPER TAPE PRINTER
A functional recreation of the PC-100C thermal printer, with dot-matrix font rendering. Copy the output as plain text or as a high-resolution bitmap image.

PRESET FILES
Load .ti59 and .ti58 state files to instantly preset the calculator with a program and data registers — useful for sharing programs or bringing old listings back to life.

DEBUGGER
Inspect program memory, variables and internal registers. Available on iPad (landscape) and Mac.

Whether you are rediscovering programs you wrote decades ago, exploring the history of handheld computing, or just curious how these machines worked, Calc-U-59 brings the TI-59 era back to life.
